Hello!

Welcome to Marine Auditory, an inspiring initiative led by passionate high school students dedicated to positively impacting the environment by raising awareness and reducing plastic pollution. As the next generation, we believe in the power of youth to drive change and create a sustainable future for all.

Join us on our mission to create a greener world! Explore our resources, get involved in our events, and connect with like-minded individuals who share our vision. Whether you're interested in organizing local clean-up initiatives, or simply learning more about plastic pollution, Marine Auditory provides a platform for you to make a difference.
